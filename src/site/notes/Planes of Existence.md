# Planes of Existence
The three Planes of Existence are varied metaphysical modes of being which span all the realms and the universe. Each [[Realms\|realm]] exists in one form in the Plane Material, in another in the Plane Beyond, and yet another in the Plane Astral. Only incorporeal beings may travel from one plane to another, and corporeal beings may project an incorporeal form into another plane.

Each plane has it's own "rules," which determine the natural circumstances in that plane. These rules are also often referred to as "modes of being."

Each realm exists in the planes material, astral and beyond simultaneously. To any place in the material plane, there is a counterpart in the planes astral and beyond which are morphed by the rules of each plane.
# The Plane Material
The Plane Material - also called the Prime Material Plane - is where mortals live.
### Rules of the Plane Material

- **Corporeal:** Everything in the material plane has a corporeal form, meaning everything can be interacted with by corporeal beings. The only exception to this are incorporeal forces such as the laws of physics, thermodynamics and the weave.
- **Weave and ley-lines:** The Weave, which is the energy that fuels all magic, flows through the material plane via ley-lines. Ley-lines, which can shift from place to place over time, transport Weave energy along themselves, releasing the energy into the world, and that's magic, baby. Places where ley-lines intersect are extremely magically potent, and magical effects and spells present in those areas may be enhanced.
- **Time:** The movement of all things in the material realm in relation to one another is known as time. Time is constantly in motion, flowing relatively steadily onward - although mortals are fond of finding ways to speed it up and slow it down using magic or psychedelics. Time exists in two modes in the material realm: objective and subjective. Objective time is the natural movement of all things in relation to one another. It has a set momentum and moves steadily on at a set pace. Subjective time is the way in which mortals experience time, which may be slowed down or sped up through various means.
- **Entropy:** Everything in the material plane has a finite amount of energy, which fuels the existence of all things - mortals, nature, stars. This energy is expended to keep all things alive. When it runs out, the thing it was fueling dies.
- **Gravity:** Gravity is the force that binds everything in the material plane together, so realms and such stay together and don't drift apart.
- **Magnetism:** Fuckin' magnets, how do they work?
- **Physical laws:** Then there's all the other laws of physics I can't be bothered to get into.
# The Plane Beyond
The Plane Beyond - also called the Underworld, the Beyond, the Nether World and the Home of Lost Souls - is where souls go when they are relieved of their corporeal forms.

[[Spirits#Kalmynn, Shepherd of the Dead\|Kalmynn]], the corvid spirit who shepherds the dead, travels from the plane beyond to the plane material to find the dead and take their souls from the material plane to the underworld.

[[Spirits#Azadrias, Alchemist of Darkness\|Azadrias]], progenitor of all necromancy, keeps the necrotic energy that permeates the plane beyond flowing, using the death energy of the preserved souls in the underworld as fuel.

[[Spirits#Ya’an Ori, Caretaker of the Underworld\|Ya'an Ori]], caretaker of the underworld, nurtures the souls brought to the underworld until their energy in the underworld is depleted. Then, the souls ready to be reincarnated, at which time they're sent back to the material plane to inhabit a new body. Once in the material plane again, the physical body a soul inhabits reinvigorates the soul with life energy.

An energy known as [[Cyclical Essence\|cyclical essence]] flows through the underworld. This energy is part of the Numens' being, and fuels the cycle of death and rebirth throughout the planes and realms.
### Rules of the Plane Beyond

- **Semi-corporeal:** Everything in the plane beyond has a corporeal form, but its inhabitants - dead souls and spirits - are incorporeal.
- **Necrotic:** Necrotic energy permeates the plane beyond, making it uninhabitable to mortals.
- **Souls and Spirits:** The plane beyond is inhabited by the souls of the dead and the spirits who nurture and preserve them. The plane is inhospitable to any being that is neither soul nor spirit.
- **Cyclical:** Souls are preserved and returned to the plane material when the time is right. Souls of the dead contain death energy, which is used in the plane beyond. When all this energy is used up, souls are returned to the material plane where they gain life energy from the bodies they inhabit.
- **Time:** Time moves in the plane beyond in accordance to the flow of objective time in the material plane.
- **Semi-magical:** The Weave is extremely weak in the plane beyond. Abjuration and Divination magic is impossible in the plane beyond, and all other magic is severely weakened. Only necrotic magic isn't weakened by the plane beyond - instead, it is greatly enhanced by [[Spirits#Azadrias, Alchemist of Darkness\|Azadrias]], progenitor of all necromancy.
# The Plane Astral
The Plane Astral - also called the Astral Sea - is where the consciousnesses of the [[The Gods of Gada#The Numen\|Numen]] reside. Although the Numen exist in each plane simultaneously, their existence is still subject to the metaphysical rules of each plane, and their conscious being can only exist in the astral plane, just as their corporeal forms exist in the material plane, and their [[Cyclical Essence\|cyclical essence]] exists in the plane beyond.

The Numen sing in the astral plane. The song is quiet and droning, barely shifting tune and without any semblance of melody, as the Numens' voices are drawn out over thousands of years of subjective time. The Numen, of course, experience it in their own subjective time - they may be singing a hearty jig in *prestissimo* for all we know!

Mystics attempt to make contact with the Numen by projecting themselves into the astral plane. They spend hundreds of thousands of years of subjective time only to decipher one word from the consciousness of the Numen. By this time, the mystic is already old and decrepit, and must pass their findings on to younger mystics - if they're even intelligible anymore.

The astral plane is a vast and formless place, much like oceans in the material plane. Beings here exist as pure energy, consciousnesses intact without any sort of form - although beings here can create incorporeal forms for themselves with the power of thought alone.

The astral plane appears to an observer as a vast expanse of stars, nebulae and other cosmic bodies swirling and floating through cosmic space. These are all incorporeal illusions of the universe, but this means that the astral plane can be used to observe various parts of the universe.
### Rules of the Plane Astral

- **Incorporeal:** The astral plane and all beings in it are completely incorporeal - corporeal things *cannot exist* in the astral plane.
- **Directionless:** The astral plane only contains space that can be moved through insofar as beings in it exist relative to one another. A being in the astral plane alone can "swim" through the expanse and feel as if they are moving, but in reality they are going nowhere. Two or more beings, however, can move relative to one another, creating or closing distance between each other.
- **Timeless:** The passage of objective time is stretched out infinitely in subjective time, meaning that a thousand years may pass for a being in the astral plane when only a second of objective time has passed in the material plane.
- **Telepathic:** There are no physical bodies in the astral plane. All beings exist as pure energy and thought alone.
- **Anti-magical:** Spells cannot be cast and magic of the Weave has no effect in the astral plane.
- **Beyond the cycle:** The cycle of life, death and rebirth, fueled by entropy, does not exist in the astral plane. It is eternal.
