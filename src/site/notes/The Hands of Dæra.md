# The Hands of Dæra
The corporeal body of the Numen god [[The Gods of Gada#Dæra, the earth\|Dæra]] forms all the earth-matter on Gada. Of this body, only Dæra's [[The Face of Dæra\|face]] and hands breach the surface of the ocean, the corporeal body of the Numen god [[The Gods of Gada#Môrin, the sea\|Môrin]].

The three hands reach out heavenward from points in the sea far away from each other. Seafarers have long used these hands to orient themselves on their voyages as two of the hands lie conveniently east and west from Dæra's face and third hand.
