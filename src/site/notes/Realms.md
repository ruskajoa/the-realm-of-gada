# The Realms
A realm is a habitable place with unique natural properties and physical laws, comprised of the bodies of [[The Gods of Gada#Those on Earth\|Those on Earth]]. Travel between realms is possible via portals, which mortal beings can step through to transport their corporeal forms from one realm to another.

One may be able to tell which realm one is in by looking up at the night sky: different [[Constellations\|constellations]] may be seen from each realm, depending on the realm's position in the universe relative to the stars which form the constellations.

Dear reader, you may think of realms as equivalent to planets in our universe. Gada is merely one of many.
# Realm travel
Travel through realms is extremely resource-intensive, but utterly possible. As the realms all exist in the same universe, a being could theoretically leave their own realm's atmosphere and float through space to another one - a theory which the wizard Raleigh perished in the attempt of. No, portals are the go-to traversal method for the inter-realm tourist.

A portal connects two places in separate realms by sending a link from one place to the other and closing the cosmic space between them within the link into one point which can be stepped through. The realms themselves stay in place and the cosmic space between is undisturbed, but the process is incredibly delicate - if two portal links intersect, it could have disastrous consequences. Luckily, the universe has gods to prevent that sort of thing from happening.

## The Hells
The fiends known as Devils come from here.

No stars can be seen from the Hells. Together with the Abyss they are known as "the starless realms." The Hells used to be connected to the Abyss until the universe was ravaged by Eramet and the Hells split apart from the Abyss and was sent soaring away from the Abyss to the furthest reaches of the universe. The two realms remain connected, however, through a wormhole, and the fiends from each realm battle each other in an eternal conflict known as the Blood War. This wormhole is the battleground of the Blood War, and it is an extremely volatile and dangerous part of cosmic space.
