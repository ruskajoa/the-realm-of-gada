# The Night Sky Throughout the Realms
The stars of the universe that float between the realms make up constellations. In the night sky, the stars are clearly visible, and civilizations throughout all the realms have looked up at them since time immemorial and imagined the stars to form certain shapes that they have then named. Mortals like to imagine great beings living in the night sky, far away in space - and in fact, some of these constellations are actual living beings roaming through space!

All the constellations together - or more accurately, all the *stars* - form the bodies of the [[The Gods of Gada#The Celestials\|Celestials]], the most ancient of all the Numen. Twinkling in the night sky, they gaze down on the realms from far above - all except Eþri, who is always looking away no matter which realm one beholds them from, gazing at something faraway and unknown.

## Constellations of the Cosmic Ones
Although they are still part of the bodies of the Numen, these constellations are formed by cosmic creatures roaming in space. They exist as independent beings and as part of the Numens' bodies simultaneously.
#### The Aboleth
Can be seen from the [[Realms#The Far Realm\|Far Realm]]
#### The Archer
A noble cosmic protector, said by some to be the elven goddess [[The Gods of Gada#High Goddess Elviathar\|Elviathar]] herself, defending Gada from cosmic threats. She is always fending off the Dragon, her cosmic nemesis.

Can be seen from [[Realms#The Realm of Gada\|Gada]].
