# The Unobservable World
Neither a [[Planes of Existence\|plane]] or a [[Realms\|realm]], but a secret third thing. The Unobservable World - also called the Pale, the Aether, or simply Yonder - is where [[The Gods of Gada#The Pantheon\|the Pantheon]] resides. Gods can reach out from here to the Observable World to influence mortals and meddle in mortal affairs by transmitting energy from their world to ours, but they cannot pass through to the Observable World, even as projections. Gods show themselves to mortals by causing visions and hallucinations in mortals' minds.

The Unobservable World and the Observable World are completely separate, the Pantheon's ability to reach out from the former to the latter to send energy through being the only exception. There is no way to pass from one to the other.
