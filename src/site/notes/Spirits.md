# Spirits
Spirits are an important part of the cycle of life, death and rebirth which constantly turns throughout all the Observable World. They are beings who can freely move between the [[Planes of Existence\|planes of existence]], and as such are not-quite-corporeal. Spirits govern the planes and how they connect.
#### Azadrias, Alchemist of Darkness
Azadrias is, according to legend, the ghost of the ancient wizard Raleigh, who created various dark magicks such as necromancy. He resides in the [[Planes of Existence#The Plane Beyond\|Plane Beyond]], using the souls of the dead there to keep these magicks alive.
#### Kalmynn, Shepherd of the Dead
Kalmynn is a corvian figure who appears to people only once in their lifetimes - at the very end. When it is time to go, one will hear the chilling music of Kalmynn’s flute, and feel the icy touch of their hand. Kalmynn guides the dead to the [[Planes of Existence#The Plane Beyond\|Plane Beyond]], and as such is simultaneously a respected but ultimately dreaded figure.
#### Ya’an Ori, Caretaker of the Underworld
Ya’an Ori is the caretaker of the [[Planes of Existence#The Plane Beyond\|Plane Beyond]], where the spirits of the dead rest. It is their duty to make sure that the spirits are given appropriate resting places after their deaths, so that they do not sleep restlessly, and to make sure that no spirits escape the underworld.
#### Elhymperiu the Ancient
An ancient force at the heart of the [[Planes of Existence#The Plane Astral\|Plane Astral]], unseen, unfathomable. At the very end of the universe, Elhymperiu will crawl out of its hiding place and devour the universe from the inside.
