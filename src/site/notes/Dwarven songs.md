# Songs of the Dwarves
Of the many Dwarven customs, telling stories through songs was among the most hallowed of them all. Their cavernous mountain homes made their singing voices echo and boom dramatically, which dwarves used to navigate those often labyrinthine halls and find one another. Feast-halls and communal spaces were also constructed with great acoustics in mind first and foremost, as singing was an important communal practice of the dwarves - nary a feast went by without many songs sung 'til voices went hoarse.
