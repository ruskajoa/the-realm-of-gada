### Welcome, dear reader, to the Realm of Gada!
Find here the collected lore of the mythical realm of Gada, my home-brew fantasy world tailor-made for TTRPGs like Dungeons & Dragons.
# Cosmology
The Realm of Gada, just like our home of Earth, is but one of many worlds in a vast, inter-connected universe teeming with life. Seek with the tag *#cosmology* and ye shall find lore beyond merely Gada itself - or click here to quickly delve into the lore of [[The Gods of Gada\|Gods]], [[Realms\|Realms]] or [[Planes of Existence\|Planes of Existence]].
# Lands
Gada is home to many a vibrant land, each one rich with cultures and tales. The lands of Gada take form from its four great continents: Odarinn in the north, Cascadia to the east, Vedallún to the west, and the thousand islands of Anned-þem in the south. Dear reader, you may scour the lands of Gada via the *#lands* tag.
# History
Gada's shared history and the unique histories of it's lands may all be sought with the tag *#history*. Hundreds of years of lore cascading into itself through time in an endless fractal pattern. Perhaps, dear reader, you'd care to begin with the history of one of Gada's most fabled lands, [[History of Elteveldt\|Elteveldt]]?
