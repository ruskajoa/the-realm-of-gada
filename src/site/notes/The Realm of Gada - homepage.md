### Welcome, dear reader, to the Realm of Gada!
Find here the collected lore of the mythical realm of Gada, my home-brew fantasy world tailor-made for TTRPGs like Dungeons & Dragons.
# Cosmology
The Realm of Gada, just like our home of Earth, is but one of many worlds in a vast, inter-connected universe teeming with life. Seek with the tag *#cosmology* and ye shall find lore beyond merely Gada itself - or click here to quickly delve into the lore of [[The Gods of Gada\|Gods]], [[Realms\|Realms]] or [[Planes of Existence\|Planes of Existence]].
# Lands
Gada is home to many a vibrant land, each one rich with cultures and tales. The lands of Gada take form on it's two great continents, the [[The Face of Dæra\|Face of Dæra]] and the southern [[The Hands of Dæra#The southern hand\|Hand of Dæra]], as well as the islands between and around the two.

The continents are literally the face and one of the hands of the Numen god [[The Gods of Gada#Dæra, the earth\|Dæra]], the physical earth of Gada. Two more of Dæra's hands also reach out of the sea, his fingers reaching up to the heavens like great towers, but these fingers are nigh-impossible to live on. The rest of him is submerged in the ocean, the body of the Numen god [[The Gods of Gada#Môrin, the sea\|Môrin]].

Dear reader, you may scour the lands of Gada via the *#lands* tag.
